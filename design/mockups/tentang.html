TENTANG

Siapa kami

Komunitas Anak Belajar adalah komunitas pendidikan bagi anak-anak prasejahtera di dua wilayah Jakarta — Cakung dan Ciawi. Kami mendampingi anak-anak agar dapat belajar, tumbuh cerdas, dan mandiri di komunitasnya sendiri.

Kegiatan kami

Setiap minggu anak-anak mengikuti kegiatan belajar — Senin dan Kamis di Cakung, Jumat di Ciawi. Kami juga menyelenggarakan program menu sehat, wisata edukasi, serta pelatihan wirausaha bagi remaja dan ibu.

Who we are

Children Learning Community provides education for underprivileged children in two areas of Jakarta — Cakung and Ciawi. We walk alongside the children so they can learn, grow, and become independent within their own community.

What we do

Each week the children take part in learning sessions — Mondays and Thursdays in Cakung, Fridays in Ciawi. We also run a healthy-meal program, educational field trips, and entrepreneurship training for teenagers and mothers.
